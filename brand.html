Brand Identity

The QualitetVerse brand should feel like a fusion of TikTok's creativity, Stripe's trustworthiness, Shopify's simplicity, and Notion's clean intelligence.

✓ Do
  • Use gradient text for key headlines
  • Apply glassmorphism to cards and panels
  • Use Space Grotesk for all headings
  • Maintain deep space black backgrounds
  • Apply subtle purple glow to interactive elements
  • Use neon cyan for CTAs and highlights
✗ Don't
  • Use pure white backgrounds
  • Use harsh, flat colors without gradients
  • Mix the old "Qualitet" gold/green palette
  • Use red or orange as primary accents
  • Apply heavy drop shadows (use glow instead)
  • Use serif or display-only fonts for UI
Comparable feeling to: TikTok — social energy Stripe — technical trust Shopify — commerce power Notion — clean intelligence

Logo

The QualitetVerse logo is a bold V-mark forming a galaxy orbit ring, symbolizing the convergence of quality and the universe of commerce.

Logo concept: V-Galaxy Ring

The V symbol represents "Verse" (Universe). The orbit ellipse surrounding it represents the ecosystem of commerce revolving around quality. The purple-to-cyan gradient flows from the brand's primary Electric Purple to the secondary Neon Cyan — symbolising innovation transforming into clarity.
